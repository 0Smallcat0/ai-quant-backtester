# v1.0.0 - Initial Release

## Highlights

### AI-Powered
- **Integrated OpenRouter/OpenAI**: Seamless connection to LLMs for strategy generation.
- **Anti-Lookahead Bias**: Built-in static analysis to detect and prevent lookahead bias in generated strategies.

### Robust Engine
- **T+1 Execution**: Realistic simulation of next-day open execution.
- **Fractional Shares**: Support for crypto and fractional trading.
- **Bankruptcy Protection**: Automatic liquidation logic to prevent negative equity.
- **Liquidation Logic**: Robust handling of margin calls and stop-outs.

### Professional Analytics
- **Portfolio-Weighted Monte Carlo**: Advanced simulation fixed to prevent numerical instability.
- **Log Scale Risk Chart**: Better visualization of exponential growth and drawdowns.
- **Smart VaR**: Value at Risk calculation based on simulation data.

### Multi-Market
- **Dual Market Support**: Full support for both US/Taiwan Stocks and Crypto markets.
- **Watchlist Management**: Easy management of tracked assets.

### Flexible Config
- **Global Settings**: Customizable initial capital, commission rates, and slippage models.
